Cross-judge finding (2023 data, n=97)

→ Page count: More pages is associated with lower grant probability across all judges and motion types (OR=0.57, 95% CI [0.33–0.99], p=0.047). The only statistically significant finding in the current dataset.
→ Longer motions: Motions with more RCW citations and case citations trend positive but results are not yet statistically significant (n too small).
→ Filing lead time: Directionally positive (earlier = higher grant rate) but high variance at current sample sizes.

Based on logistic regression across all contested outcomes with complete features. All findings are correlational, not causal. Wide confidence intervals reflect small sample.
