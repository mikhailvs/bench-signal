What We Found

  • High overall grant rate (93%). Of 697 motion outcomes linked, 643 were granted — suggesting family law motions in Spokane courts are almost universally uncontested or pro forma at the hearing stage.
  • Substantial judge-level variation. Grant rates among judges with sufficient outcome data range from 87% to 100%, a ~13 percentage-point spread that persists across motion types.
  • Dissolution dominates the docket. 66% of cases are dissolutions; contempt and modification hearings (14% combined) show slightly lower grant rates and greater motion complexity.
  • Outcome linking is the key bottleneck. Only ~11.7% of all documents have linked outcomes; OCR quality on form-filled PDFs is the primary constraint — see Methodology.
