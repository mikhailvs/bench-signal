Observations

  • October peak. October had the highest monthly outcome count (72), closely followed by April and September — consistent with post-summer filing surges.
  • Tuesday dominates. Tuesday had the most linked outcomes, likely reflecting docket scheduling patterns rather than substantive differences.
  • Hearing time not captured. Scheduled hearing times were not scraped in the 2023 dataset. The hour-of-day chart has been suppressed to avoid displaying a data artifact.
  • February dip. February had the lowest count (40), consistent with fewer court days and post-holiday filings not yet reaching hearing stage.
